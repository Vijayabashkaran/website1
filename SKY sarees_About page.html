About us

We Work Hard To Provide You The Best Quality Sky Sarees and Successfully, also Offer Direct Manufacturer Prices

SKY Sarees&textiles.com is a special online platform for you, particularly for women because we are dealing with the highly skilled Sky area small scale weavers who are masters in weaving all type of silk sarees – Indian Bridal Sarees, silk cotton sarees, Indian Traditional Sarees, Handloom Sarees, Silk Sarees, Art Sarees and many more.

We procure Silk Sarees directly from the weavers who offer high quality product with traditional and latest designs. Hence we offer Silk Sarees at a very significantly competitive price and we are proud to bring these hidden talents to the forefront and offer them a platform online.

We also cater to Wholesalers, Retailers and Resellers as we offer a very competitive price compared to the big Retail Showrooms and other sellers due to our direct link with the skilled and untapped weavers of Sky sarees.

How are we dealing with weavers and how we produce SKY sarees?

We choose the Saree designs and colors, offer threads and design patterns and present them to the weavers, who in turn produce 100% best quality silk sarees, which is offered to you at best prices. As we deal directly with the weavers, we are able to give you the best competitive price offers with high quality, premium design sarees.

We visit SKY area weavers every day and are happy to promote their hand made small scale works online through our website. It is our way of empowering these small scale handloom weavers by showcasing their Premium quality Silk Cotton, Soft Silk, Fancy, Bridal Silk Sarees,
